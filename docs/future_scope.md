## Risk Mitigation & Contingency

### Identified Risks
1. **Regulatory Changes** - Frequent updates in medicine regulations
   - Mitigation: Legal compliance team, regulatory monitoring

2. **Competition** - Entry of large e-commerce players
   - Mitigation: Focus on healthcare specialization, partnerships

3. **Technology Changes** - Rapid tech evolution
   - Mitigation: Modular architecture, continuous learning

4. **Market Volatility** - Economic downturns
   - Mitigation: Essential product positioning, cost management

5. **Cybersecurity Threats** - Healthcare data breaches
   - Mitigation: Advanced security, regular audits, insurance
