# Problem Statement

## Overview
The healthcare and pharmaceutical industry in India faces significant challenges in connecting medicine buyers with verified sellers efficiently. There is a need for a modern, trustworthy digital platform that streamlines the medicine procurement process.

## Key Challenges

### 1. **Fragmented Supply Chain**
- No centralized platform connecting customers, pharmacies, and medicine distributors
- Multiple intermediaries lead to higher costs and longer delivery times
- Difficulty in tracking medicine authenticity and sources

### 2. **Trust & Authenticity Issues**
- Customers struggle to verify medicine authenticity before purchase
- No standardized verification system for vendors (GSTIN, Drug License)
- Counterfeit medicines pose serious health risks

### 3. **Limited Discovery & Accessibility**
- Customers cannot easily search for medicines across multiple sellers
- No intelligent recommendations based on health conditions
- Pricing comparison is difficult and time-consuming

### 4. **Inefficient Ordering & Delivery**
- Manual order processing leads to delays
- No real-time tracking of orders
- Limited visibility into inventory and stock availability

### 5. **Vendor Challenges**
- Pharmacies and distributors lack a digital platform to reach customers
- Manual inventory management is error-prone
- Limited analytics on sales and customer behavior

## Solution: MedBroker

MedBroker is a **B2B/B2C medicine marketplace platform** that solves these problems by:

✅ **Connecting**
- Direct connection between verified vendors and customers
- Eliminates unnecessary intermediaries
- Reduces costs and delivery time

✅ **Building Trust**
- Thorough vendor verification (GSTIN & Drug License)
- Transparent vendor ratings and reviews
- Secure payment gateway with encrypted transactions

✅ **Enabling Discovery**
- Advanced search and filtering capabilities
- AI-powered medicine recommendations
- Real-time inventory visibility

✅ **Streamlining Operations**
- Automated order processing
- Real-time order tracking
- Analytics dashboard for vendors
- Digital invoice generation

✅ **Improving Experience**
- 24/7 AI chatbot support
- Multiple payment options
- Fast delivery (24-48 hours)
- Role-based access (Customer, Vendor, Admin)
